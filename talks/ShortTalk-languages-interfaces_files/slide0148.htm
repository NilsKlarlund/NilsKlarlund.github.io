Introducing ShortStep
nShortStep is my 7th or so attempt at producing a foot keyboard that
nCovers virtually all control functions
nShft-tab, Alt-tab, Alt-Shft-Tab, left, Ctrl-b, Shft-left, PgUp, Ctrl-Alt-s, Ctrl-Esc, Backspace, …
nDoesn’t hurt
nIs fast (foot pedals on an organ are spaced far apart)
nDoesn’t require you to take your socks off
